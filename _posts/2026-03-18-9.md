Dorkbot Manchester #9 will be happening next week on Wednesday, March 18th! With talks and demos from...
### [SIMON BLACKMORE](https://simonblackmore.net/)
L’UPIC Ludique is a new electronic instrument. With a minimal wooden interface and coloured pencils as controllers, users can draw and manipulate raw audio waveforms.
### [NØELLE](https://www.instagram.com/noellenoelleno/)
Motion reactivity for live immersive experiences. Exploring Touchdesigner and Ableton with MediaPipe, Flowfal and the MiMU Gloves.
### [WILL SEGERMAN](http://www.willsegerman.com/)
A non-linear speedrun through 7 years of engineering strange things for escape rooms.
### [HELIOS](https://solarwindchi.me/) 
Solar Windchime installation and talk. Not the one in Victoria Baths 
### VERDE01001 // PULSO
Freddy Mendoza AKA [FENO](https://www.instagram.com/feno1000000/) unveils a post-human interface merging differential growth geometry with living moss. The installation transforms biological matter into a sonic trigger.
